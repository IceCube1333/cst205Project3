David Stern:
Energy Economics in Support of Climate Policy

Economic Growth:

Working with economic historian Astrid Kander to determine the role of energy in
long-run economic growth and the Industrial Revolution by developing a uniﬁed
growth model and testing it against historical data.

Substitution:

Developing better estimates of substitution possibilities between fuels and between
energy and capital using meta-analysis, econometrics, and CGE models (G-Cubed).

Technological Change:

Follow on from EERH project - determining factors affecting macro-level adoption of
energy efﬁciency and pollution abatement technology.

Climate:

° Collaboration with Simon Hay (Oxford) — testing climate/Malaria trends in E. Africa.
- Upcoming IPCC AR5 role.
